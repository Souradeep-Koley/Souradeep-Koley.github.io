About

I am Souradeep Koley, a final-year PhD scholar at the Department of Management Studies, IIT Roorkee, working in the intersection of Operations Management (OM) and Management Information Systems (MIS). My work centers on the platform economy, such as ridehailing systems and online delivery systems, aiming to enhance gig workers' livelihoods while improving firms' long-term efficiency.
